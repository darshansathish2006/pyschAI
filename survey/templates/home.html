Mental Health Assessment Platform

Welcome to the Mental Health Assessment Platform

This platform helps you assess your mental well-being using scientifically recognized questionnaires.
You can choose between different assessment methods based on your preference.

Please select one of the following assessment tools to begin:

PHQ-9 (Patient Health Questionnaire-9)

A quick and widely used 9-question screening tool that helps identify the severity of depression symptoms over the past two weeks.

Start PHQ-9 Assessment

BDI (Beck Depression Inventory)

A more detailed questionnaire designed to assess the presence and intensity of depressive symptoms.

Start BDI Assessment

Note: This tool is intended for educational purposes only and is not a medical diagnosis. Please consult a qualified mental health professional for proper evaluation and treatment.
